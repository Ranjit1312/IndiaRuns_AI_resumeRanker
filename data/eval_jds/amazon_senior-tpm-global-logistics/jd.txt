Senior Technical Program Manager, Amazon Global Logistics (AGL) — Amazon
Location: IND, TS, Hyderabad | Job ID: 10415620

Description
As a Senior Technical Program Manager for Amazon Global Logistics (AGL), you'll lead complex cross-organizational initiatives that revolutionize e-commerce through innovative global logistics solutions. You'll drive programs that help break down barriers for selling partners, enabling them to reach hundreds of millions of customers worldwide through our highly-available, scalable distributed systems.

Key job responsibilities
- Lead and manage complex technical programs across multiple engineering teams and stakeholders
- Design and implement sustainable processes for cross-functional program management
- Drive development of software solutions that optimize global fulfillment and cross-border selling
- Establish and track key performance indicators (KPIs) and service level agreements (SLAs)
- Manage program scope, schedule, budget, quality, risk, and critical path elements
- Coordinate release schedules and ensure successful delivery of technical solutions
- Partner with engineering teams to build scalable, distributed systems
- Present progress and metrics to senior leadership

Basic Qualifications
- 5+ years of technical product or program management experience
- 7+ years of working directly with engineering teams experience
- 3+ years of software development experience
- 5+ years of technical program management working directly with software engineering teams experience
- Experience managing programs across cross functional teams, building processes and coordinating release schedules

Preferred Qualifications
- 5+ years of project management disciplines including scope, schedule, budget, quality, along with risk and critical path management experience
- Experience managing projects across cross functional teams, building sustainable processes and coordinating release schedules
- Experience defining KPI's/SLA's used to drive multi-million dollar businesses and reporting to senior leadership
